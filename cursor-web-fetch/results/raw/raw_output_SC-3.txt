This is a list of countries and dependencies by population. It includes [sovereign states](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sovereign_state), inhabited [dependent territories](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dependent_territory) and, in some cases, constituent countries of sovereign states, with inclusion within the list being primarily based on the [ISO](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Organization_for_Standardization) standard [ISO 3166-1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_3166-1). For instance, the [United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom) is considered a single entity, while the constituent countries of the [Kingdom of the Netherlands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_the_Netherlands) are considered separately. In addition, this list includes certain [states with limited recognition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_states_with_limited_recognition) not found in ISO 3166-1. Also given in a percentage is each country's population compared with the [world population](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_population), which the [United Nations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations) estimated at 8.232 billion as of 2025.

## Method

Figures used in this chart are based on the most up-to-date estimates or [projections](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Population_projection) by the national [census](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Census) authority, where available, and are usually rounded off.

Where updated national data are not available, figures are based on the estimates or projections for 2024 by the Population Division of the [United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Nations_Department_of_Economic_and_Social_Affairs).

Because the compiled figures are not collected at the same time in every country, or at the same level of accuracy, the resulting numerical comparisons may create misleading conclusions. Furthermore, the addition of figures from all countries may not equal the world total.

Areas that form integral parts of [sovereign states](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sovereign_state), such as the [countries of the United Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Countries_of_the_United_Kingdom), are counted as part of the sovereign states concerned. Not included are other entities that are not sovereign states, such as the [European Union](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Union), and independent territories that do not have permanent populations, such as the [Chagos Archipelago](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chagos_Archipelago) and [various countries' claims](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Territorial_claims_in_Antarctica) to [Antarctica](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antarctica).

## Explanatory notes

The last census was conducted in 1979. Sources disagree about the current population:

- The US Census Bureau provides an estimate of 49,552,566 for 2025.
- The CIA gives an estimate of 40,121,552 for 2024.
- The BBC gives a figure of 38.3 million for 2023.
- The [Encyclopædia Britannica](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Encyclop%C3%A6dia_Britannica) gives an estimate of 36,432,000 for 2025.
- The Afghani [National Statistics and Information Authority](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Statistics_and_Information_Authority) gives an estimate of 35,695,527 for 2024.

Eritrea has never conducted an official government census. Sources disagree about the current population:

- [Encyclopedia Britannica](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Encyclopedia_Britannica) gives an estimate of 6,416,000 for 2025.
- The [US Census Bureau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/US_Census_Bureau) gives an estimate of 6.4 million for 2025.
- The BBC gives an estimate of 6.2 million for 2023.
- The [Eritrea Ministry Of Information](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cabinet_of_Ministers_of_Eritrea) gives an estimate of 5.8 million for 2020.
- The UN gives an estimate of 3,502,000 for 2024.
